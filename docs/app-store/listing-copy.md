## Description

Varq is a native macOS reader for the books and comics you already own.

Bring your DRM-free EPUB, PDF, and CBZ files into one calm, carefully designed library. Varq keeps the experience focused on reading: warm page tones, practical typography controls, smooth page turns, and native macOS performance.

**Read your way**

- Import EPUB, PDF, and CBZ files with drag and drop or the system file picker
- Pick a light, dark, or sepia reading page, then adjust font, size, line height, margins, and page layout
- Read CBZ comics left to right or right to left, with single-page and two-page spread options
- Resume exactly where you left off

**Keep what matters**

- Highlight selected EPUB and PDF text in Varq’s accent and neon palettes
- Attach notes to selected text or a page location
- Return from a highlight directly to its place in the book

**Keep your library yours**

- Organize books into collections and sort your library your way
- Protect selected books with Touch ID or the system authentication fallback; protected files are encrypted at rest
- No account required: your library and reading data stay on your Mac

Varq is built with Swift and SwiftUI for macOS 15 or later.

## Claims review

Use only the claims above in App Store Connect. In particular:

- Do **not** claim support for CBR, MOBI, AZW3, FB2, cloud sync, OPDS, AI features, reading goals, or an iOS app; they are not part of this release.
- Do **not** claim that highlights or notes can be exported from the app UI until that user-facing flow ships.
- Do **not** use book covers, book text, comic panels, or author names in product-page media unless their use is licensed. The screenshot plan requires original demo content.
